about our

tea station

Tea Station is a premium tea maker located in Toronto. Founded in 2012, we blend, pack and deliver our sustainably and ethically sourced fresh teas to restaurants, offices, and homes daily.

With a desire to bring our refreshing approach to tea drinkers everywhere, Tea Station has expanded its reach to hotels, workspaces and retail partners from coast to coast in Canada.
